\chapternotnumbered{Introduction} \label{chap:Introduction}

The goal of this thesis is to investigate the use of certain numerical methods in simulating atmospheric flows around topography, the so-called \emph{orographic flows.} The text is structured as follows:

\begin{compactenum}
	\item chapter \Cref{chap:FLOWS} provides an introduction to the physics of \emph{orographic flows};
	\item chapter \Cref{chap:SPH} summarizes the main numerical method used, that is the \emph{Smoothed Particles (SPH)};
	\item chapter \Cref{chap:VORONOI} introduces a different numerical method: \emph{Semi-Implicit Lagrangian Voronoi Approximation (SILVA)};
	\item chapter \Cref{chap:EXPERIMENTS} presents these numerical methods through numerical experiments and discusses the obtained results.
\end{compactenum}
